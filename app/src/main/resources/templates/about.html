Our Mission

CineTick is more than just a ticketing website; it is a student-led initiative designed specifically for the Technological Institute of the Philippines. We aim to modernize how students access entertainment on campus.

By merging creative design with Java Spring Boot, we provide an organized platform that handles everything from seat selection to real-time receipt generation.
